# Caira — Next Moves (prioritized)

*Companion to `STRATEGIC_REVIEW_V2.md` and `FOUNDER_TRANSITION.md`. Five high-leverage moves, each
chosen because it hits more than one of {app, income, exit} at once.*

## TLDR
**If you only do one thing this quarter: #2 — convert 2 DSW-network pilots into signed PAID
contracts.** It's the keystone: it funds the $10k founder salary (via the raise it unlocks), proves
the retention story (#3), gives the co-founder (#5) something real to sell, and de-risks the raise.
Features (#1) and compliance (#4) make pilots stick and scale; the signed paid contract is what turns
casual DSW into funded founder.

## Priority order

### 1. NDIS report / PDF export pack — *app · income · exit*
Auto-generated progress reports + audit packs from care notes.
- **App:** turns scattered notes into the artefact providers are legally required to produce.
- **Income:** switching-cost moat → higher price, lower churn.
- **Exit:** raises retention/NRR, which sets the multiple.
- Low legal risk (worker-facing). **Ship before the games layer.**

### 2. Convert 2 paid pilots in 60 days — *income · exit* — **KEYSTONE**
Turn the two warmest DSW-network leads into **paid** contracts on real data, even at a small price.
- Paid (not free) tests willingness-to-pay and proves traction to investors.
- 2 paying logos + retention data = the difference between raising a seed and not.
- This is what unlocks the $10k founder salary (salary ← raise ← traction).

### 3. Instrument retention / NRR / time-saved from day one — *income · exit*
Lightweight analytics: logins, notes created, time-to-report, active seats/sites.
- **Income:** "saves X hours/worker/week" is the sales pitch — only claimable with data.
- **Exit:** NRR is *the* number a buyer underwrites. Start the time-series early for 12+ months of
  trend by raise/exit.
- Small build, outsized payoff — makes every pitch evidence-based.

### 4. Lock the compliance core — *exit · income*
Book the NDIS/privacy lawyer: **registration classification ruling** + privacy policy, ToS, DPA,
dual-role consent.
- **Income:** a sales gate for enterprise/government buyers, not a cost.
- **Exit:** clean compliance = multiple premium + liability shield; messy compliance kills diligence.
- Already the critical path — do it early to de-risk downstream and reach bigger logos sooner.

### 5. Sign the domain-credible co-founder — *income · exit*
Commercial/clinical co-founder with NDIS sector authority (not an engineer).
- **Income:** they own sales/relationships → progress on weeks you're still doing DSW shifts.
- **Exit:** solo → 2-founder is the single biggest fundability lever; many funds won't back a solo
  founder. No co-founder = no seed = no $10k salary = no full-time.
- Give meaningful equity now; cheap equity buys a fundable, higher-valued company.

## Sequencing
Keystone (#2) first → it makes #3, #4, #5 real and urgent. #1 and #4 make the pilots stick and scale.
Everything ladders back to the transition trigger: **paid pilots → seed round → $10k founder salary →
full-time.**
