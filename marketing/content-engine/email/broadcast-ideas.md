# Caira Email Broadcast Ideas (ongoing list engagement)

After the 5-email nurture (`nurture-sequence.md`), keep the list warm with a
**roughly monthly broadcast** — value first, Caira mentioned lightly and only
when there's something real to say. Same voice as the posts: Edward, plain, AU
spelling. Same compliance rules (no NDIS endorsement, no guarantees, every email
has unsubscribe).

**Rhythm:** ~1 broadcast/month. Mix ~70% value, ~20% community/story, ~10% Caira.
Repurpose your best-performing Facebook posts — don't write from scratch.

## The idea bank

1. **"Best of the comments"** — round up the best answers from a Facebook
   community thread (price-guide confusion, best advice for new workers). Credit
   the community; it makes readers feel part of something.
2. **Monthly practical deep-dive** — one topic done properly: travel claims,
   audit-ready notes, setting your rate. Link the relevant lead magnet.
3. **"What changed this month in the NDIS"** — plain-English summary of any price
   guide / policy update. Hugely valuable and very shareable. Link official sources.
4. **Founder build-in-public update** — honest progress on Caira, a decision you
   made, what early users taught you. Disclosed, no hype.
5. **Tool/resource of the month** — a genuinely useful (non-Caira) app, template,
   podcast or group. Generosity builds trust and reciprocity.
6. **Reader question answered** — pick a real question from a reply or comment and
   answer it properly. Invite more questions.
7. **Seasonal/timely** — tax-time record-keeping (May–June), summer heat & client
   safety, end-of-year burnout and rest.
8. **Mini case study** — once you have a willing, real early user: their before/
   after in their words (with permission). No invented quotes.
9. **"Day in the life"** — a relatable story from your own shifts; ties to a tip.
10. **Template/checklist drop** — a brand-new free download (incident-note
    template, handover template). Keeps the list valuable to stay subscribed to.
11. **Community spotlight** — celebrate a member's win or milestone (with
    permission). Warm, human.
12. **Soft Caira milestone** — launch, a new feature, opening more early spots.
    Use sparingly; lead with the value it gives the reader.

## Guardrails
- Lead with what's in it for the reader; Caira is the P.S., not the headline.
- Never imply NDIS/NDIA endorsement; never guarantee compliance or outcomes.
- Real quotes only — leave testimonial slots blank until you have genuine ones.
- One clear CTA per email; keep it short and skimmable on a phone.
