## The problem (you already know it)

You finish a run of shifts, then sit down at night to write notes from memory.
The admin eats the hours that should go to the people you support. And there's
that quiet worry: would these notes actually hold up if anyone asked?

## How Caira helps

- **Capture in the moment.** A note in seconds during the shift, not an hour at 9pm.
- **The right bones, by default.** When, who, goal, what happened, response — structured for you, in plain factual language.
- **Built for one hand and a phone.** Because that's how the work actually happens.
- **Your records, organised.** Consistent, in one place, ready when you need them.

*(Caira helps you write your own compliant notes — it doesn't replace your
provider's policies or your professional judgement.)*
